About Me

As a photographer for over 20 years, I have always been a big fan of film and the process of developing it. When I came across people using coffee as a developing medium. I just has to try it just to see if it would work. I'm no chemist so I did not understand how it could work. On my first attempt with it to my surprise it worked and very well. It differently has a different feel and look then traditional developers so for that reason alone I was hooked, Plus its a lot cheaper to use. I still use traditional developers it just depends on what film and or camera im using. what developer I'll will use..


The Photo above on this page was taken with Kodak Tmax 100 Black and White film, I used my 1950s Zeiss Ikon Contina camera its a rangefinder 35mm camera the image was taken from 4feet at f8 the shutter was at 1/125 s..
